Welcome to HonourERD - A Research Prototype for Learning Normalization

Thank you for participating in this research study! HonourERD is a prototype designed to investigate whether a visual, step-by-step learning tool with semi-automated feedback can improve students' understanding of database normalization.

This tool is designed to explore how **visual learning with semi-automated feedback** can improve student understanding of **database normalization**.

What You’ll Do:

  • Enter unstructured data and follow the guided normalization process (1NF → 2NF → 3NF).
  • Receive real-time feedback at each step to help you understand the process
  • Complete a 10-question quiz at the end to assess your understanding of normalization.

Why Your Participation Matters:

This research explores how interactive, guided learning impacts students' ability to apply normalization concepts. Your progress and quiz performance will help evaluate the effectiveness of visual and automated feedback in database education.

⚠️ Important

  • You must complete all steps of normalization before taking the quiz.
  • The quiz is mandatory and consists of 10 questions testing what you've learned.
  • Your participation and feedback are valuable for improving database education tools.

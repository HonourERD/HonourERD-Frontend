Welcome to HonourERD - A Research Prototype for Learning Normalisation

Thank you for participating in this research study! HonourERD is a prototype designed to investigate whether a visual, step-by-step learning tool with semi-automated feedback can assist traditional learning to help improve students' understanding of database normalisation.

This tool is designed to explore how **visual learning with semi-automated feedback** can improve student understanding of **database normalisation**.

Why Your Participation Matters:

This research explores how interactive, guided learning impacts students' ability to apply normalisation concepts. Your progress and quiz performance will help evaluate the effectiveness of visual and automated feedback in database education.

⚠️ Important

  • You do not need to complete all steps of normalisation before taking the quiz.
  • You can quit at any time
  • The quiz is optional and consists of 10 questions testing what you've learned.
  • Your participation and feedback are valuable for improving database education tools.
